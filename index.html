About

Hello! My name is Seongjun Kang, and I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the HCIS Lab of Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology (GIST).

My vision: multisensory synchronization for advanced digital interactions

My research primarily focuses on haptic technology within Virtual Reality. I am dedicated to developing hardware and software that seamlessly integrate various human senses, enhancing multimodal sensory experiences. This involves designing innovative interfaces and exploring the potential of human somatosensory perception to create more immersive and intuitive digital interactions. By bridging the gap between the virtual and physical worlds, my goal is to contribute to a more nuanced and engaging user experience.
