About

Hello! My name is Seongjun Kang, and I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the HCIS Lab of Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology (GIST).

My vision: multisensory synchronization for advanced digital interactions
My research primarily focuses on haptic technology within Virtual Reality. I am dedicated to developing hardware and software technologies that aid in seamlessly integrating various human senses, as well as augmenting or reproducing multimodal sensory experiences.
